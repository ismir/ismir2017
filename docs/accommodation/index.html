Accommodation

We recommend ISMIR 2017 participants to stay in the following hotels nearby the conference site. Each of these hotels is within 5 to 10 minutes drive to the conference site, and also allows convenient access to attractions around Suzhou. Special rates will be applied when booking the hotels with the given reservation codes. In addition, the conference organizing committee is working on organizing regular shuttle bus services between these hotels and the conference venue.

To book, email your details (guest name(s), type/number of room(s), arrival date/time, departure date, reservation code, and any special requirements, such as a non-smoking room) to the respective hotel managers as provided. The hotel managers will confirm your booking within a few days.

Please make your booking with the hotel before August 31, 2017. Hotel room booking will be subject to availability after this date.
