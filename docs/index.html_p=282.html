Your ISMIR 2017 registration includes:

  • Your individual 2017 – 2018 ISMIR membership fee (USD $10)
  • Attendance to all ISMIR 2017 paper sessions, posters, and invited speeches between the 24th – 27th October 2017
  • ISMIR 2017 coffee breaks
  • ISMIR 2017 lunch tickets
  • 1 ticket to the ISMIR 2017 Welcome Reception on October 23, 2017
  • Attendance to the ISMIR 2017 Garden Concert together with a guided cruise on the Grand Canal on October 25, 2017
  • 1 ticket for the ISMIR 2017 Banquet and Community Performance Night on October 26, 2017
  • Possible T-shirt from sponsors
  • An electronic version of the ISMIR 2017 Conference Proceedings

Important:

  • Please note that, for each paper/musical piece, at least one author must be registered for the conference by August 4, 2017. If none of the authors is registered by this date, the paper/music piece will be removed from the conference proceedings and programme.
  • One regular registration covers at most two submissions (the author registered must be an author of both contributions).
  • One student registration covers at most one submission (the student registered must be an author of the contribution). You must provide proof of your student status and your advisor’s information (including name and email) to get the student discount. Please send it to ismir2017-registration@ismir.net (post-docs are not eligible for student rates).
  • Cancellation Policy:
    You may cancel your registration by writing to ismir2017-registration@ismir.net. In order to receive a refund, the request for cancellation must be received by the ISMIR2017 Registration Chair on or before 30 September 2017, with a processing fee of USD 50. If your cancellation request is received after 30 September 2017, no refund will be given.

Invitation letter:

If you need an invitation letter, after completing your registration and payment, send a request by e-mail to jinjing.kong@nusri.cn, katrina.xu@nusri.cn.

If you have any other questions about registration, please contact ismir2017-registration@ismir.net.
